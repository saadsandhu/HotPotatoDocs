# Hot Potato Bot Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 2, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what information Hot Potato Bot ("the Bot") collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information. By using the Bot, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

The Bot may collect and store the following information as required for its functionality:

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server (Guild) IDs
* Discord Channel IDs (when required for bot features)
* Economy data (such as virtual currency balances)
* Inventory and purchased items
* Achievements and gameplay statistics
* Suggestions submitted through the Bot, along with userId, username, serverId
* Configuration settings for servers

The Bot does **not** collect:

* Discord passwords
* Email addresses
* Payment information
* Direct Messages, except those intentionally sent to the Bot for its features (such as suggestions)
* Any information outside of what is necessary to provide its functionality

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used solely to operate and improve the Bot, including:

* Providing game and economy features
* Saving user progress
* Managing inventories and achievements
* Supporting moderation features
* Storing server-specific settings
* Processing commands and interactions
* Responding to suggestions submitted through the Bot

## 4. Data Sharing

Your information is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.


## 5. Data Storage

Data is stored only for as long as necessary to provide the Bot's functionality. Some data may remain until it is manually deleted or the Bot is removed from a server.

## 6. Data Deletion

If you would like your stored data removed, you may contact the Bot developer.

Removing the Bot from a server may also result in the removal of server-specific data where applicable.

## 7. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 10. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy or requests relating to your data, please contact the Bot developer through Discord @saad2007.
